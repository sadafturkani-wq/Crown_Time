The Submariner is the archetype of the diver's watch. Born in 1953 and continuously refined, it pairs unparalleled water resistance with the unmistakable presence of a Rolex Oyster.

  • Cerachrom unidirectional rotatable bezel
  • Caliber 3235 movement, 70h power reserve
